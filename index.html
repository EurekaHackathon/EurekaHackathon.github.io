ABBEY PARK 'S 2023 HACKATHON
Welcome to Eureka! where the next generation of tech innovators come together to collaborate, create, and showcase their skills. Come to Abbey Park High School to innovate, solve real world problems, and meet new likeminded people. Each team will create a solution or project related to our theme, and get the chance to win prizes. This event is dedicated to empowering and inspiring high school students to pursue their passion in technology and design. With workshops, coding challenges, and networking opportunities, participants will have the chance to unleash their full potential and bring their ideas to life. So gear up, get ready to code, and let's build the future together!
